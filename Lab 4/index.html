Welcome to Jao Barbershop

Welcome to Jao Barbershop, located in the heart of Ottawa. With over a decade of experience, our team brings a level of expertise and passion that sets us apart.

To our loyal Clients - We extend our heartfelt gratitude for your continued support and trust in our services.

To our new Clients - Our commitment is to listen to your needs and offer expert advice on the latest trends and styles, ensuring you leave feeling confident and satisfied.

Service description

Here is a list of services offered

Haircut

Our skilled barbers provide precision haircuts tailored to your desired style and preference. Whether you're looking for a classic cut or a trendy new look, we've got you covered.

Beard Trim

Elevate your grooming routine with our professional beard trimming service. From shaping to detailing, our barbers will sculpt your beard to perfection, enhancing your overall appearance.

Shave

Experience the ultimate shaving experience with our traditional hot towel shave. Sit back, relax, and let our skilled barbers provide you with a smooth and refreshing shave using high-quality products and techniques.
